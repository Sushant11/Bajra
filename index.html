About Us

Bajra Automobiles was established in 2009 as one of the authorized dealer of Honda 2 wheelers in Nepal. Our prime objective is to increase Honda 2 wheeler sales with main focus on customer service and satisfaction. Over the years, Bajra Automobiles has been the number 1 Honda Dealer as one of the best seller of Honda 2 Wheelers in Nepal and are confident that it will continue as time goes on with dedicated staffs, multiple outlets around Kathmandu and our special services. Keeping our valuable customers in mind, we have been providing services such as bike finance, exchange and insurance as required.
